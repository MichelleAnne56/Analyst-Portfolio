# Introduction to Russian Public Sentiment Project  
The first research product is an examination of public sentiment in Russia about the top problems facing the country with data from the Global Barometer Surveys. This public survey data includes 1205 observations from Russian citizens in 2021 which show that corruption and economic issues dominate as the top national concerns in Russia. My approach to this data was to start with This report looks at five questions in the survey: (1) What are the most important problems facing Russia that the government should address? (2) How widespread do you think corruption and bribe-taking are in your local/municipal government? (3) How widespread do you think corruption and bribe-taking are in the national government? (4) How would you rate the overall economic condition of Russia today? and (5) How fair do you think income distribution is in Russia? All of these questions are public survey questions that have been answered on an ordinal
