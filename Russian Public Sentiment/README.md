# Introduction to Russian Public Sentiment Project  
The first research product is an examination of public sentiment in Russia about the top problems facing the country with data from the Global Barometer Surveys. This public survey data includes 1205 observations from Russian citizens in 2021 which show that corruption and economic issues dominate as the top national concerns in Russia. 

## Approach 
My approach to this reserach project was to start with the survey question about what the most important problems facing Russia that the government should address are. From there, I dove deeper into the top two issues: corruption and economic issues. To examine why so many Russians consider corruption to the top issue facing Russia, I looked at two survey questions: (1) How widespread do you think corruption and bribe-taking are in your local/municipal government? and (2) How widespread do you think corruption and bribe-taking are in the national government? To examine why so many Russians consider various economic issues to be the top problem facing the country, I looked at two more survey questions: (3) How would you rate the overall economic condition of Russia today? and (4) How fair do you think income distribution is in Russia?
